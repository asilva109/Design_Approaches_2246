If you are looking for fresh, corlourful and fruity drinks which are refreshing and perfect for the summer, look no further! Here are top 5 go-to summer drinks which are super easy to make at home and perfect for this scorching weather. All of these drinks are non-alcoholic.
Let the summer drink season begin!

1. LEMON ICED TEA



For lemon iced tea, start by adding water in a saucepan and cook with tea leaves and sugar for 4-5 mins. Strain and let it cool. To assemble, add ice cubes, strained tea mixture, lemon juice, 4-5 mint leaves and lemon slices.



2. WATERMELON MOJITO



For watermelon mojito, start by blending freshly cut watermelon to get some juice. Muddle mint leaves on the side. To assemble, add ice cubes, lemon juice, muddled mint, lemon slices, sugar and watermelon juice. Give it a good mix.



3. MANGO LASSI



For mango lassi, in a blender add homemade dahi, fresh mango cubes, sugar, cardamom powder and ice cubes. Blend until everything comes together.



4. MOHABBAT KA SHARBAT



For mohabbat ka sharbat, in a bowl combine milk, sugar, rose syrup and freshly cut watermelon cubes.



5. PAAN MILKSHAKE



For paan milkshake, in a blender add ice cubes, milk, whole paan, saunf or fennel seeds, desiccated coconut, vanilla ice cream and green food colour.
