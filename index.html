Health Tips





Summer is here! A change of season calls for a change in routine, a revamped diet, and of course, a new exercise regimen that suits the weather. Hover over the images to learn more about the simple summer health tips that will help you make the most of the summer season without compromising on your health. Also, checkout the colour of the selected text.

Wear Sunscreen

Apply sunscreen before going outdoors and re-apply every 2 hours to protect your skin from the sun and to avoid getting sunburns.

Choose the right liquids

Try to avoid hot and hard drinks and drink enough water. Choose refreshing drinks like juices and stay hydrated.

Stay fit

Engage in refreshing outdoor activities such as swimming, yoga, walking etc to keep your body and mind aligned.

Eat healthy

Enrich your diet with healthy fruits, vegetables and good amount of healthy lean protein. Avoid spicy food.

Give yourself a break

Taking time off from work, even for a long weekend, allows body to replenish and repair itself. Give your family and yourself that much needed vacation.

Sleep well

Pay attention towards developing a good sleep cycle by keeping the same bedtime and wake-up schedule.
